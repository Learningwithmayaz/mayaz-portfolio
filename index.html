Research Focus

I am a researcher in Civil and Environmental Engineering with expertise in Finite Element Analysis (FEA), Machine Learning, ArcGIS, and Abaqus-based structural and environmental simulations. My research focuses on developing robust computational models, leveraging advanced machine learning methodologies, and integrating AI-driven analytical tools to solve complex problems in civil and environmental infrastructure.

Committed to data-driven research and sustainable engineering solutions, I have contributed to projects involving environmental impact assessment, hydrological modeling, and eco-friendly infrastructure design. My expertise also includes structural health monitoring, smart material characterization, and computational mechanics.
